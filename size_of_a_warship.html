Corvette

The corvette is the smallest type of warship. They are manuverable and fast, generally ranging in speed from 25-30 knots, and armed with missiles and low-caliber guns. Generally speaking any nation with a sizeable budget and shipbuilding capability can make a corvette of some kind. Corvettes are usually deployed in ASW, patrol, escort, and coastal defense roles.
