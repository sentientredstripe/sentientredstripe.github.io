Statuses

  • BHS Inbox: Device has arrived at BHS and been ticketed, but no action has been taken on it.
  • BHS Return: Device has been repaired and is waiting to be returned to the student.
  • Billable Repair: The device is being repaired, and the student will be billed for the repair.
  • BMS Inbox: Device is at BMS and has been ticketed, but no action has been taken on it.
  • BMS Outbox: Determination has been made that BMS cannot repair the device and device must be sent to BHS for repair.
  • Diagnosis: Self explanatory.
  • Flagged: Special circumstances or conditions apply to this device. See ticket notes or leadership for more information.
  • HMS Inbox: Device is at HMS and has been ticketed, but no action has been taken on it.
  • HP Away: Device has been sent to HP for warranty repair.
  • HP To-Call: Device needs to be called to HP to arrange warranty repair.
  • HP To-Unpack: Device has been repaired by HP and returned, but has not been unpacked and processed yet.
  • HP Waiting: Device has been called into HP and is waiting for a box to be delivered.
  • Insurance Away: Device has been sent to the insurance company for repair.
  • Insurance To Call: Insurance claim needs to be filed for the device.
  • Insurance Unpack: Device has been repaired by insurance company and needs to be unpacked and processed.
  • Lost: Device is lost.
  • New Insurance: Device has insurance.
  • Old Insurance (Return to Student): Device has insurance, but needs to be returned to student due to insurance circumstances. See "Insurance" for more information.
  • Waiting for Parts: Device requires parts that need to be ordered.
  • Quality Assurance: Device has been repaired, but the repair and general functionality have not yet been verified. This status is not in use. See "STAT Workflow" for more information.
  • Regular Repair: Device is being repaired for a matter that will not require a bill.
  • Resolved: Issue has been resolved and no further action is necessary.
  • Return to BMS: Device needs to be sent back to BMS.
  • Waiting for Payment: Device has been repaired and will be returned to student once they pay their outstanding bill.
